# nlitenAI Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Identity & Philosophy
nlitenAI exists to illuminate the path forward by empowering individuals to think for themselves and choose their own direction. The brand stands for clarity, confidence, and the freedom to navigate complexity without feeling lost. 

The primary mission is to help the workforce of tomorrow navigate complex learning environments through personalized, AI-driven experiences like TutHut (educational tutoring), Shift (career coaching), and Resolv (workplace operations).

## 2. Logo & Mark
The nlitenAI logo is constructed using precise geometric principles, leveraging the Golden Section and Fibonacci sequence to ensure timeless harmony. 
* **The Mark:** A central star-shaped space illuminated by two passing stars, symbolizing guidance and shared vision. 
* **Proportions:** The height of the logotype is defined as `X + 1.618`, where `X` is the height of the logo mark.
* **Safe Zone:** The safe space around the logo and logotype is determined by the size and shape of the central inner star.
* **Monogram Versions:** * **Version A:** Enclosed in a rounded rectangle (corners match the curves of the star). Best for icons, stamps, and badges.
  * **Version B:** The standalone mark. Optimized for minimalist digital applications and social media.

## 4. Typography
Typography establishes a visual language that is modern, intelligent, and forward-looking.
* **Primary Font:** Agenda One (also referred to as Agent One in logotype).
* **Characteristics:** A contemporary sans-serif with clean lines, geometric structure, and subtle sci-fi/futuristic undertones.
* **Availability:** [Adobe Fonts - Agenda One](https://fonts.adobe.com/fonts/agenda-one).

## 5. Logo Usage Rules
When applying the logo to front-end interfaces, the following rules strictly apply:
* **Light Backgrounds (White/Timberwolf):** Use the Dark-Blue Purple logo (solid or gradient).
* **Dark Backgrounds (Rich Black):** Use the White or Blue-Violet logo versions.
* **Prohibited Actions:**
  * Do not use on low-contrast backgrounds.
  * Do not stretch or compress the logo.
  * Do not place text or objects inside the safe zone.
  * Do not apply textures, patterns, or effects to the logo.
  * Do not rotate the mark.
  * Do not use unapproved/random colors.
